First Day of Class

 2023-01-31

First day was today. Instructor was fantastic, though I feel bad because I didn’t really have any questions to ask. A lot of what was covered I already knew about from my previous stints on and off the previous several years futzing with programming, and reading in general trying to keep up with what is out there. He did cover about two months worth of material in the span of two and a half hours or so!

It’s only going to get faster from here, and I’m ready for it. I’m ready for a new change of pace. A new direction; somewhere that I can dive off from and know that I’ll get somewhere better instead of being stuck in a no-where spot.
